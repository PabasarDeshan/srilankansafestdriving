Abseiling - Flying Ravana Adventure Park (Ella Sri Lanka)

18 June 2022

Rappelling down a rockface whilst enjoying the stunning views of Ella rock and the Ravana waterfall will leave you in absolute awe.

This controlled descent will be guided by trained & skilled climbing instructors using certified and approved gear to deliver you a wonderful experience.

In order to assure your safety, there are height & weight requirements we expect you to meet.

What is included

  • Personal Protective equipment
  • Safety Lockers
  • Safety Instructions & training
  • Instructors

Flying Ravana Abseiling restrictions

Minimum Weight: 30 kg

Maximum Weight: 90 kg

Age: 16 years and above

Clothing

Wear clothes that are not too loose and baggy (you don’t want to get them caught on anything).

Skirts and dresses are not recommended. Bring something to tie your long hair.

Weather: During thunderstorms and heavy rains Abseiling operations will be kept temporarily on hold.
